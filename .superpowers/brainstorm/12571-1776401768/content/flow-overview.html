Key Design Decisions

Phone Frame Wrapper
iPhone-style bezel with notch, status bar, and home indicator. Screen-record the browser window for authentic mobile feel.
Tap-to-Advance
Each screen has interactive elements. Tap buttons/cards to advance. Screen 5 (Publish) has working dropdowns and toggles to pause on.
Animated Transitions
CSS slide transitions between screens. Progress bars animate on video gen and upload steps. Feels like a real app.
Brand-Matched
Uses your exact BRAND colors from config.ts. Dark theme, coral/teal/amber accents throughout.
